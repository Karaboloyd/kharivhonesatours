About Us

Exclusive, Authentic Tours

At Kharivhone, we craft unforgettable experiences with the help of locally sourced guides who bring South Africa's stories to life. From breathtaking landscapes to vibrant cultures and abundant wildlife, every tour is designed to leave lasting memories.

Our mission is simple: to showcase the beauty, heritage, and diversity of South Africa through immersive, guided journeys. With years of experience and a passion for sharing our homeland, we ensure that every tour is not only enjoyable, but also educational and inspiring.

Explore What We Offer

Discover South Africa like never before with our carefully curated tours. From vibrant cities and cultural landmarks to breathtaking wildlife and heritage sites, each journey promises unforgettable experiences.

All tours include comfortable transportation, knowledgeable guides, and entrance to the attractions — so you can simply relax and enjoy the adventure.

Johannesburg and Soweto Half-day Tour

Experience the beating heart of South Africa, where history and modern life merge. Visit Soweto, home to Nelson Mandela and Desmond Tutu, and see iconic landmarks such as Vilakazi Street and the Hector Pieterson Memorial. Explore downtown Johannesburg and uncover the story of the “City of Gold.”

Book Now

Pretoria City Half-day Tour

Discover South Africa’s administrative capital, also called the “Jacaranda City” for its purple-lined streets in spring. Highlights include the Union Buildings, Voortrekker Monument, Church Square, and vibrant local markets. A journey that blends political history, culture, and natural beauty.

Book Now

Pilanesburg National Park Full-day Tour

Embark on an unforgettable safari in a volcanic crater that is home to the Big Five and over 7,000 animals. From lions to elephants and more than 600 bird species, every turn offers a chance to witness African wildlife in its natural habitat. A must-see for nature and adventure lovers.

Book Now

Lion and Safari Park Half-day Tour

Get up close with the kings of the African savannah and other majestic wildlife including cheetahs, hyenas, giraffes, and antelope. This tour offers thrilling photo opportunities and a deeper understanding of African predators and their habitat.

Book Now

Elephant Sanctuary Half-day Tour

Walk alongside gentle giants and learn about their intelligence, behavior, and conservation. This sanctuary experience allows visitors to interact with elephants in a safe and ethical environment — a truly humbling encounter with Africa’s largest land mammals.

Book Now

Lesedi Cultural Village – Half-day Tour

Immerse yourself in South Africa’s vibrant cultural heritage. Meet local communities, enjoy traditional dances, taste authentic meals, and hear fascinating stories of the Zulu, Xhosa, Pedi, Basotho, and Ndebele people. A colorful journey into the soul of the nation.

Book Now

Cradle of Humankind - Maropeng & Sterkfontein

Step back in time at this UNESCO World Heritage Site, where the story of human origins unfolds. Explore ancient caves, see fossils of our earliest ancestors, and visit the award-winning Maropeng Visitor Centre. A must for history and archaeology enthusiasts.

Book Now

Constitutional Hill & Lilliesleaf Heritage Site

Trace South Africa’s journey to democracy with a powerful visit to Constitution Hill — once a prison, now the home of the Constitutional Court. Continue to Lilliesleaf Farm, the secret headquarters of the liberation movement. A moving and educational tour for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

What should I bring on a safari tour?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, comfortable clothing, binoculars, a camera, and any personal medications. For early morning safaris, we suggest bringing a light jacket as it can be chilly.

Are your tours suitable for children?

Yes, most of our tours are family-friendly. We offer special rates for children and can customize tours to be more engaging for younger participants. Some safari tours may have age restrictions for safety reasons.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made at least 2 days before the tour date. For cancellations within 3-6 days, we offer a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations within 48 hours of the tour.

Do you provide hotel pickup and drop-off?

Yes, we provide complimentary pickup and drop-off at most hotels in Johannesburg and Pretoria. For locations outside these areas, additional charges may apply.
